# Dragon Campaign

> **War foundational TLoD** — légendaire conflit ~**11,000 ans pré-game** entre **Humans + species opprimées** vs **Winglies dictateurs**. **Diaz humain** rallies rebellion → declare war **Fort Magrad** → **Vellweb capital oppressed** + **Liberation Army training**. **Découverte canon Dragoon Spirit mechanism** : Dragon eyes merge into singular gem on death → harnessable. ## History canon

### Origine canon : oppression Wingly

- **Some time after Winglies' birth from Divine Tree** (107th fruit), **significant portion** commencent à traiter autres species comme inférieures
- **Subjugation canon** : species opprimées **forced to fight each other to the death** dans un **coliseum within Kadessa** (lore tragique, motive rebellion)
- Multiple species opprimées : **Humans + Dragons + Mininto + Gigantos + Mermaids** (probable, à confirmer)

### Naissance rebellion (Diaz humain)

- **Diaz = human canon** (originellement) — pas encore "Emperor", pas encore reborn
- **Trouve le courage** to stand in defiance to oppressors
- **Rallies fellow humans** → gathering in numbers + inciting rebellion
- **Speech canon Fort Magrad** : **formally declared war on Winglies**
- 🆕 **Diaz = leader rebellion canon** (vs antagoniste reborn final)

### Vellweb capital + Liberation Army

- **Vellweb devient capital pour les opprimés** canon (cohérent Vellweb = mausolée des 4 anciens Dragoons morts Disc 4)
- **Diaz gathers victims** → **training begins** → **Liberation Army foundation canon**
- Pattern lore : Vellweb = lieu sacré pour la mémoire de la rebellion (post-Dragon Campaign)

### Découverte Dragoon Spirit mécanisme canon

🆕 **Mécanisme canon précisé** (vs wiki Dragons + Dragons fandom) :

- **Humans discover secret about Dragons**
- **Specifically** : **when Dragons die, their eyes merge into a singular gem** ⭐
- 🆕 **Cette merge des eyes (1-7) = explication eye count canon** ! Eye count = puissance du Dragon = puissance du Spirit
- **If harvested at the moment of a Dragon's death** → Dragoon Spirit **activated**
- **Used by sentient wielders** who can "**tame the beast within**" canon language
- → Rebellion gains rivalry power vs Wingly magic = freedom path
- **Thus it became known as the Dragon Campaign** (name origin canon)

### Réponse Wingly canon

- Find threat serious
- **Already harnessed magical power of Virage Embryo via Crystal Sphere** canon (108th fruit soul mécanisme)
- **Learn to spawn Virage at will** without birthing the embryo
- Use Virage as answer to rebellion's Dragoons + Dragons

### Guerre canon — Liberation Army campaigns

- **Led by Diaz**, Dragoons fight alongside Liberation Army
- **5 floating Wingly cities** = cibles war canon (noms ? probable à investiguer)
- **Flanvel = floating mobile fortress** canon (cohérent **Tower of Flanvel Disc 3** = vestige de cette fortress ? Kashua Glacier site)
- **One by one**, each falls

### Final battle Kadessa canon

#### Setup

- **Last battle Kadessa** (Wingly capital)
- Dragoons fight **many Virage + at least one Super Virage**
- Goal : sack the capital

#### Kanzas sacrifice canon

- **Kanzas (Vellweb ancien Dragoon, Violet/Thunder)** **sacrifices himself to clear Super Virage** canon
- 🆕 **Confirme** : Kanzas mort par self-sacrifice vs Super Virage (cohérent fandom Belzac trivia "Kanzas combat Super Virage")
- Implication data-model : Kanzas final move = self-destruct/sacrifice ability ?

#### Michael (Rose's vassal Dragon) Dragon Campaign role canon

- **Present** during final battle
- **Defeats many Virage in aerial melee combat**
- **Uses special laser to eliminate several at once**
- Implication : Michael **major asset** Dragon Campaign final battle
- Cohérent canon fandom : Michael "fought in Dragon Campaign" + later becomes savage → killed by Rose

#### Zieg vs Melbu Frahma duel canon

- **Zieg engages dictator Melbu Frahma directly**
- **Setup canon** : Zieg's **Dragoon form** vs Melbu's **Crystal Sphere + Dragon Buster** ⚠️
- 🆕 **Dragon Buster wielded by Melbu Frahma** canon (vs Wingly anti-Dragon weapon vs Dragoons rebellion)
- **Zieg strikes fatal blow** → Melbu fatally wounded
- **Last-ditch survival Melbu** :
  1. **Petrifies Zieg** canon
  2. **Embeds his essence within the Dragoon Spirit** (Red-Eye DS)
  - Implication : Melbu Frahma soul survives **inside Red-Eye Dragon Spirit** canon → cohérent Zieg possession Disc 4 reveal
- **They both fell** → mutual fatal blow result

#### Kadessa destabilisation + Crystal Sphere broken

- Kadessa **begins to destabilize and crumble**
- **Crystal Sphere broken** → soul du 108th fruit (Virage Embryo) **released** → début **tragic new cycle in search of Moon Children** canon
- 🆕 **Précision canon** : la rupture Crystal Sphere = mécanique exacte qui lance le **cycle 108 ans Moon Child** (cohérent Divine Tree canon)

#### Belzac + Shirley + Zieg deaths canon (scène finale tragique)

- **Belzac stops structure from crushing himself + Shirley** (column held up via Dragoon power + will)
- **Virage impaled his entire chest** (mortal wound)
- Virage charges **primary laser**
- **Shirley knocks magical arrow aimed at Virage** (canon archer Shirley)
- **Zieg's petrification overtakes him** → utters final words → tells **Rose to save herself**
- → **Belzac dies + Shirley dies + Zieg "dies" (petrified)** = scène finale canon

> Note canon : Belzac + Shirley sacrifices ensemble Kadessa cohérent avec [Belzac.md](../bosses/Belzac.md) backstory ("saves Shirley from collapsing roof, Virage laser kills both"). Fandom Dragon Campaign donne narrative complète et résout contradictions précédentes.

### Résultat

- **Victory for humans + all oppressed species** canon
- **At great cost** :
  - **4 anciens Dragoons morts** : Belzac (Gold), Damia (Blue-Sea), Syuveil (Jade), Kanzas (Violet) → souls à Vellweb
  - **Shirley dead** (mais Spirit à Shrine of Shirley séparé)
  - **Zieg "dead"** (petrified, soul embedded Red-Eye DS, Melbu's essence inside)
  - **Rose survives immortelle** (Choker mechanic)
  - **Diaz "dies"** mais reborn 11,000 ans plus tard canon (cf. Disc 1 Greham reveal + Doel "received power from reborn Diaz")
- Winglies unseated → **Human Age begins** canon
- Cycle Moon Child started (compensation tragique)
