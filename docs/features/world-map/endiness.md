## History canon (master Endiness)

### Age of Winglies (>11,000 ans pre-game)

1. **Initial state** : world **completely barren + devoid of life**
2. **Soa plants seed** → **Divine Tree grows** → **108 fruits** = unique species each
3. **107th fruit = Winglies** canon — born intelligence + vast magical power, capable wondrous feats
4. **Winglies suspended birth of 108th species** canon (cohérent Crystal Sphere harness Virage Embryo)
5. Some Winglies = peace/equality vision ; **many treated other species as playthings**
6. Humans found **Holy Empire of Gloriano** as **seat of rebellion** canon
7. Dragoon Spirit discovery (eye-merge canon) → war prep

### Dragon Campaign (~11,000 ans pre-game)

- **War backwash magical power** **laid waste to Gloriano** → **changed landscape to deadly desert = Death Frontier** canon ⚠️ confirme + précise
- **Large island south ripped apart** → **Broken Islands** canon ⚠️ NEW canon location
- Climax : **Dragoons assault Kadessa Royal Capital** → crashed to ground
- Most Dragoons died, Wingly dictator (Melbu Frahma) defeated
- **Surviving Winglies surrender** + grant Humans **3 magical artifacts** (= 3 Divine Moon Objects canon)
- **Winglies hide with magic** (fear hostility/annihilation) → cohérent Forest of Winglies + Aglis underwater + Ulara hidden + Zenebatos canon

### Age of Humans (Present Day = TLoD time)

- Humans divide continent into **4 regions** :
  - **Serdio** (Disc 1 — Basil north + Sandora south civil war)
  - **Tiberoa** (Disc 2 — Donau Flower City + Fletz King Zior + Twin Castle)
  - **Mille Seseau** (Disc 3 — Deningrad capital, Sacred Sisters, state religion Divine Tree worship)
  - **Gloriano** (north-west, partial = Death Frontier south, partial ruins north)
- **Death Frontier left untouched** (uninhabitable post-scorch)
- **Gloriano north** retains **ruins canon** (vestige Holy Empire ancient)
- 11k ans relative peace until **Serdian War re-ignited Disc 1** (Doel/Diaz/Lloyd conspiracy)
